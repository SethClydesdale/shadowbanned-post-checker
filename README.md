### How to Use the Offline Version
1. [Download](https://github.com/SethClydesdale/shadowbanned-post-checker/archive/refs/heads/main.zip) this repository.
2. Unzip the files.
3. Open **index.html** in the browser of your choice.

※Please note that you still need an internet connection to fetch tweets for testing.


### Why make or Use this?

X is not transparent to its users on if your posts (or account for that matter) has been shadowbanned or deboosted. As such, I took the initiative to make a tool that checks your posts for a possible shadowban. This lets users decide if they need to delete their post and repost it to reach a wider audience and avoid being deboosted.

For example, say you have Post 1 and Post 2. Post 1 gets your usual interactions, but for some reason Post 2 has not gotten any interactions for hours. The reason Post 2 didn't get any interactions is because it was mistakenly flagged for "Adult content" and thus was shadowbanned/deboosted. You wouldn't know the reason for this, however, unless you used this tool to check, because X doesn't tell us that its purposely deboosting your posts.
